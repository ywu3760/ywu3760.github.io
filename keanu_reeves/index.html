KEANU
REEVES
Birth
This actor was born on September 2, 1964, in Beirut, Lebanon to a family of diverse origins: English, Hawaiian, Portuguese, and Chinese.
Tough Childhood
Despite the incredible family heritage of diversity, Keanu' father was a victim of substance abuse and was sent to jail for selling heroin when Keanu was very young.
Going Places
Following his parents' divorce in 1966, Keanu and his mother traveled to Sydney and New York before finally settling in Toronto. As a result, Keanu's school experience was very tough because as one would imagine, he would have to move to a new place before he could even make a friend at school.
First Hit
In spite of these hardships in life, Keanu practiced acting since age 9 and was able to find himself in several Hollywood films. However, his first major hit came with "Bill & Ted's Excellent Adventure." The film was a hit and everyone praised his performance.
Another Hit
Following several years of performing as a bassist in a band, Reeves returned to the acting industry and acted in several films. In his first blockbuster film, "Speed", Reeves finally became noted as a star.
The Matrix
The one thing that everyone knows about Keanu is that he performed in the movie, "The Matrix", which was banger. With this movie, Keanu made it clear that he belonged to the top of Hollywood actors.
Tragedy
While Keanu was working on sequels to "The Matrix", an unfortunate car accident happened to his girlfriend, Jennifer Syme, which ended up killing her. Many speculated that Jennifer was depressed at this time because their stillborn daughter unfortunately die. Keanu was extremely overwhelmed by what happened and he took look break from the acting industry to grief before returning to work.
New Business
After performing low-profile roles in several films following the Matrix, Keanu began his own motorcycle company, Arch Motorcycle Co., to pursue his side-interest in motorbikes.
John Wick
Just like his success in "The Matrix", Keanu made another hit film with th production of "John Wick". The fans loved the idea of an ex-hit man avenging his pet dog.
A New Relationship
After years of refusing to find a new partner following the death of his first girlfriend and child, Keanu decided to try it again with Alexandra Grant, hoping that the past incidents won't occur again.
